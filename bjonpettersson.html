    ABOUT ME


    I am a 3rd year student at The Royal Danish Academy of Fine Arts
    Schools of Architecture, Design and Conservation (KADK), in Copenhagen.
    My field of study is architecture with specialization in design, where
    focus lies on the detail as a part of a totality. I am interested in
    exploring how an architectural theme can work in different scales and
    for different purposes, with ex. shape, texture, furniture and construction details.
