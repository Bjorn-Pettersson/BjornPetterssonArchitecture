    EN TILL EN

    The assignment was to make a small house that was livable with an area of 30m2.
    My approach was to make a small cottage that could be placed in a more private
    setting than a usual house. The most notable feature of the cottage may be the semi
    transparent walls. The idea behind was to integrate the house into the surroundings,
    not just from the outside but also from the inside. The concept is that the light inside
    will match the light outside during the day, and at night the opposite will happen.
    The interior light will flow out around the house.
